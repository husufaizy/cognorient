A simple path to understanding your cognitive baseline.

In just a few easy steps, you can start building a valuable picture of your cognitive health over time.

1. Set Your Baseline

Start with a few simple exercises to establish your personal cognitive baseline. This helps in creating a personalized tracking experience.

2. Daily 5-Min Check-in

Spend just 5 minutes each day with engaging, science-backed games that are both fun and insightful. Consistency is key!

3. Track Your Progress

View easy-to-understand reports of your performance. You can share these insights with your family or healthcare provider.

Engaging Activities, Powerful Insights.

Our activities are designed to be fun and simple, while targeting key cognitive functions like memory and attention.

VISUAL MEMORY

Challenge with Pattern Match

Remember the sequence of shapes and then tap them in order. This classic memory game is a fun way to exercise your short-term visual memory and concentration skills.

  • Boosts concentration and focus.
  • Tracks visual pattern recognition.
  • Adaptive difficulty for a continuous challenge.
AUDITORY MEMORY

Test with Story Recall

Listen carefully to a short story. When it's finished, simply retell it in your own words. This activity helps monitor auditory memory and verbal recall abilities.

  • Enhances listening and comprehension skills.
  • Measures ability to recall details.
  • Privacy-focused: audio is processed on-device.

Our Scientific Approach

Cognorient is designed with principles from cognitive science to provide meaningful insights. Our activities are inspired by common methods used in preliminary cognitive screenings.

Important Information

Cognorient is a screening tool to help you track cognitive patterns. It is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace consultation with a healthcare professional. The app is designed to empower you with information for discussions about your health.
